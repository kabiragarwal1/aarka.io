# Global Media Management Platform Blueprint

## Goal
Build a full-circle platform for small business owners to:
1. Create content once.
2. Publish across social, OTT, display, and AI placement channels.
3. Auto-manage performance when enabled.
4. Analyze results and iterate.
5. Stay HIPAA/GDPR compliant by default.

## Full-circle workflow
1. **Create**: Build creative assets, choose campaign objective, set tone and target audience.
2. **Translate & Localize**: Auto-generate region/language variants with localized CTAs.
3. **Compliance Review**: Enforce HIPAA/GDPR checks and policy guidance before launch.
4. **Publish Worldwide**: Push to selected channels/regions from one plan.
5. **Auto-Manage (Optional)**: Apply budget and creative optimizations automatically.
6. **Analyze**: Track impressions, CTR, conversions, and spend.
7. **Adjust & Repeat**: Use recommendations and auto-actions to improve outcomes.

## Core modules
- **Campaign Studio**: content creation, planning calendar, approval workflows.
- **Distribution Hub**: channel connectors for social, OTT, display, AI inventory.
- **Translation Engine**: multilingual generation + human review options.
- **Compliance Engine**: HIPAA/GDPR safeguards, audit logs, consent checks.
- **Analytics & Optimization**: real-time KPIs, AI recommendations, closed-loop actions.

## HIPAA/GDPR Do's and Don'ts
### Do
- Collect explicit consent for personalization in GDPR jurisdictions.
- Pseudonymize user identifiers for analytics.
- Keep immutable logs for who changed what and when.
- Restrict high-risk categories and regions with policy guardrails.

### Don't
- Include PHI in ad creative or audience definitions.
- Store personal data longer than declared retention policies.
- Transfer personal data cross-border without legal safeguards.
- Let auto-optimization target prohibited segments.

## Suggested roadmap
- **Phase 1 (MVP)**: campaign CRUD, translation stub, compliance checks, basic analytics.
- **Phase 2**: channel adapters, consent manager integration, optimization rules engine.
- **Phase 3**: advanced attribution, MMM support, region-level legal policy packs.
